# HR Analytics Dashboard Results

## Executive Summary
- Total Employees: 500
- Dataset covers 10 departments and 12 months of attendance records.
- Built for workforce intelligence, attrition analysis, employee health score, productivity index, and HR action recommendations.

## Planned Dashboard Insights

### 1. Executive Dashboard
Tracks total employees, active employees, attrition rate, attendance rate, average salary, and average performance.

### 2. Employee Health Dashboard
Uses attendance, performance, and training data to classify employees into Low Risk, Medium Risk, and High Risk groups.

### 3. Attrition Dashboard
Highlights attrition by department, tenure, salary band, job role, and reason for leaving.

### 4. HR Action Center
Identifies employees who need training, promotion candidates, high-risk employees, and business recommendations.
